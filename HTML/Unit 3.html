Unit 3

A digital portfolio is an onscreen way to showcase your
achievements to potential employers or when applying for a
course.
Digital portfolios can be viewed by anyone with a computer and
an internet browser.
I learned how to create a digital portfolio that includes a series
of web pages with links to content that I had created.
Also I learned how to make use of multimedia assets such as
images, sound and video to make your portfolio appealing
and engaging.
