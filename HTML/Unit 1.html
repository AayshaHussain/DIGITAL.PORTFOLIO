Unit 1

In this unit I gained a understanding of the main
technologies and processes behind the internet and
investigated how they came together and how it let
us view websites and send information around the
world.
Considering a range of devices, including
smartphones and digital music players, I investigate
the technology that enables digital devices to share
and exchange information. Also in this unit there was
an exam which was based on everything we learnt
from this unit.
